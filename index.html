WALKER CREEK RANCH
Marin County Outdoor School & Retreat Center

Since 1990, Walker Creek Ranch has been a place for people to connect with each other, their communities, and nature. Each year, we serve 5,000 students in our overnight outdoor education programs. We host an additional 5,000 guests each year for private retreats, family reunions, weddings, and more. We pride ourselves as a community that welcomes all people to experience the beauty of Wild West Marin.
